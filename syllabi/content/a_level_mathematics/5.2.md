# 5.2 Permutations and combinations

1. understand the terms **permutation** and **combination**, and solve simple problems involving selections
2. solve problems about arrangements of objects in a line, including those involving repetition (e.g. the number of ways of arranging the letters of the word 'NEEDLESS') and restriction (e.g. the number of ways several people can stand in a line if two particular people must, or must not, stand next to each other)
