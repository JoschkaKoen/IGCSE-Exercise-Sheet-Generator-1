# 3.6 Numerical solution of equations

1. locate approximately a root of an equation, by means of graphical considerations and/or searching for a sign change
2. understand the idea of, and use the notation for, a sequence of approximations which converges to a root of an equation
3. understand how a given simple iterative formula of the form $x_{n+1} = F(x_n)$ relates to the equation being solved, and use a given iteration, or an iteration based on a given rearrangement of an equation, to determine a root to a prescribed degree of accuracy
