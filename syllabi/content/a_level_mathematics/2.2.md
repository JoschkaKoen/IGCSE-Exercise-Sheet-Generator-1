# 2.2 Logarithmic and exponential functions

1. understand the relationship between logarithms and indices, and use the laws of logarithms (excluding change of base)
2. understand the definition and properties of $e^x$ and $\ln x$, including their relationship as inverse functions and their graphs
3. use logarithms to solve equations and inequalities in which the unknown appears in indices
4. use logarithms to transform a given relationship to linear form, and hence determine unknown constants by considering the gradient and/or intercept
