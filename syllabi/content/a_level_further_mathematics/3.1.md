# 3.1 Motion of a projectile

1. model the motion of a **projectile** as a particle moving with **constant acceleration** and understand any limitations of the model
2. use horizontal and vertical equations of motion to solve problems on projectile motion, including the magnitude and direction of the velocity at a given time or position, the range on a horizontal plane and the greatest height reached
3. derive and use the **Cartesian equation** of the **trajectory** of a projectile, including problems in which the initial speed and/or angle of projection may be unknown
