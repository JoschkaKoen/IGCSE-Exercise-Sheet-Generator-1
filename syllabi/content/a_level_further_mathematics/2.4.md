# 2.4 Integration

1. integrate hyperbolic functions and recognise integrals of functions of the form $\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}}$, $\dfrac{1}{a^2 + x^2}$ and $\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2 - a^2}}$, integrating associated functions using trigonometric or hyperbolic substitutions
2. derive and use reduction formulae for the evaluation of definite integrals
3. understand how the area under a curve may be approximated by areas of rectangles, and use rectangles to estimate or set bounds for the area or to derive inequalities or limits concerning sums
4. use integration to find arc lengths (Cartesian, parametric or polar) and surface areas of revolution about one of the axes
