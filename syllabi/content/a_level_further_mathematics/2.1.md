# 2.1 Hyperbolic functions

1. understand the definitions of the hyperbolic functions $\sinh x$, $\cosh x$, $\tanh x$, $\operatorname{sech} x$, $\operatorname{cosech} x$, $\coth x$ in terms of the exponential function
2. sketch the graphs of hyperbolic functions
3. prove and use identities involving hyperbolic functions
4. understand and use the definitions of the inverse hyperbolic functions and derive and use the logarithmic forms
