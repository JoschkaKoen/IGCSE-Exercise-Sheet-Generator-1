# 1.5 Polar coordinates

1. understand the relations between **Cartesian** and **polar coordinates**, and convert equations of curves from Cartesian to polar form and vice versa
2. sketch simple **polar curves**, for $0 \leqslant \theta < 2\pi$ or $-\pi < \theta \leqslant \pi$ or a subset of either interval
3. recall the formula $\tfrac{1}{2}\int r^2\,d\theta$ for the area of a sector, and use this formula in simple cases
