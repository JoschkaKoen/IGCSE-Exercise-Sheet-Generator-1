# 4.4 Non-parametric tests

1. understand the idea of a **non-parametric test** and appreciate situations in which such a test might be useful
2. understand the basis of the **sign test**, the **Wilcoxon signed-rank test** and the **Wilcoxon rank-sum test**
3. use a single-sample sign test and a single-sample Wilcoxon signed-rank test to test a hypothesis concerning a population median
4. use a paired-sample sign test, a Wilcoxon matched-pairs signed-rank test and a Wilcoxon rank-sum test, as appropriate, to test for identity of populations
